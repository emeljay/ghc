Activity 1

Set up

  1. Download workshop materials here, if you haven't already.
  2. Open the workshop materials folder and navigate to the website/ directory.
  3. Open index_embedded_screenreader.html in Chrome. NOTE: If you feel comfortable using a screen reader already on your machine, open index_no_screenreader.html instead.

Exercises

  1. Try using the keyboard (tab key) to buy an item.
  2. Try determining the price of an item. Does the screen reader tell you the price of items as you navigate?
